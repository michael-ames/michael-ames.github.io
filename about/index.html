About me

As a design researcher, I’ve helped media companies discover that mindless entertainment is anything but mindless.

I’ve shown clients in the tech sector how consumers expend effort to intensify and extend their own positive emotional experiences.

And I’ve helped clients working in healthcare, government, and education feel potent, not cowed, when faced with a mountain of data.

How I work

I employ qualitative, ethnographic, usability, and design research methods in my work. I also draw heavily on my training in anthropology, psychology, and the study of emotions in product and experience design.
